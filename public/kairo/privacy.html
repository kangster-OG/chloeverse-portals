Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Kairo: Peptide/GLP tracker (“Kairo,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the Kairo mobile application and related services.

Kairo is a private peptide and GLP protocol tracker. It is designed for personal record-keeping, protocol organization, reminders, progress tracking, inventory context, widgets, and review workflows. Kairo does not provide medical advice, diagnose conditions, prescribe treatment, recommend dosing, sell medications, source peptides, or replace guidance from a licensed healthcare professional.

By using Kairo, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you choose to provide, information needed to operate the app, and limited usage information that helps us maintain and improve Kairo.

Account Information

If you create an account or use sync features, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • User ID or account identifier
  • Authentication information needed to sign in and secure your account

Protocol, Health, and Fitness Information

Kairo may store information you enter into the app, including:

  • Peptide, GLP, or protocol names
  • Dose, cadence, route, schedule, and reminder information
  • Shot logs, injection sites, side effects, notes, and adherence history
  • Inventory, vial, supply, concentration, and runway information
  • Weight, body metrics, progress records, habit logs, and weekly review information
  • Optional lab or health-context fields you choose to enter
  • Any other health, fitness, or protocol-related information you choose to save in Kairo

You decide what information to enter. Kairo is intended to help organize records you already choose to track.

Photos and User Content

If you use photo or evidence features, we may collect and store:

  • Progress photos
  • Uploaded images
  • Notes, logs, and other content you add to the app

We do not publicly display your photos or user content without your permission.

Apple Health Information

If you choose to connect Apple Health, Kairo may request permission to read selected health or fitness data, such as weight or body measurements, to support your tracking experience.

Apple Health access is optional. You can control Apple Health permissions at any time in iOS Settings. Kairo does not use Apple Health data for advertising or cross-app tracking.

Purchase Information

If you purchase a subscription, Apple processes your payment through the App Store. We may receive subscription status, product identifiers, purchase history, renewal status, entitlement status, and related transaction information needed to unlock paid features and provide support.

We do not receive or store your full payment card number.

Product Interaction and Usage Data

We may collect limited information about how features are used, such as onboarding progress, subscription screen interactions, feature usage, app version, timestamps, and general app activity.

This information helps us understand whether Kairo is working properly, improve the product, debug issues, and maintain subscriptions and account features.

Device and Technical Data

We may collect technical information needed to operate and secure the service, such as:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash logs
  • Performance information
  • Approximate timestamps of app activity

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain Kairo
  • Save and display your protocol, shot, inventory, progress, and review records
  • Sync your data across sessions or devices when sync is enabled
  • Authenticate your account
  • Provide subscription access and manage entitlements
  • Send reminders, widgets, shortcuts, and app-related notifications when enabled
  • Improve app reliability, performance, and user experience
  • Respond to support requests
  • Protect against fraud, abuse, unauthorized access, and security issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. No Cross-App Tracking or Ad Attribution

Kairo does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

Kairo does not use the App Tracking Transparency permission, does not access the advertising identifier for tracking, and does not share your data with third-party advertising networks for cross-app tracking or ad attribution.

We do not sell your personal information.

4. How We Share Information

We may share information only in the limited situations described below.

Service Providers

We may use trusted service providers to help operate Kairo, such as authentication, database hosting, cloud sync, subscription infrastructure, crash reporting, analytics, or customer support tools.

These providers may process information only as needed to provide services to us and are not permitted to use your information for their own advertising purposes.

Apple

Apple processes App Store purchases, subscriptions, refunds, and payment information according to Apple’s own terms and privacy practices.

Legal Compliance and Safety

We may disclose information if required to comply with law, legal process, government requests, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of Kairo, our users, or others.

Business Transfers

If Kairo is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, or sale of assets, information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to this Privacy Policy or a policy that provides similar protections.

With Your Consent

We may share information when you direct us to do so or give us permission.

5. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide Kairo, maintain your account, support subscriptions, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Protocol records, health-related entries, photos, notes, and other user content are retained until you delete them, delete your account, or request deletion, unless we are required to retain certain information by law.

Backups and logs may persist for a limited period as part of normal security and recovery practices.

6. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your information
  • Export or receive a copy of your information
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent
  • Object to or restrict certain processing

You may be able to manage some information directly in the app. You may also contact us to request access, correction, export, or deletion.

7. Account and Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data. Deletion may include account information, protocol records, shot logs, progress records, photos, notes, and other user content associated with your account.

Some information may be retained where necessary for legal, security, fraud prevention, subscription, or accounting purposes.

8. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Because Kairo may contain sensitive health and protocol information, you should use a secure device passcode and keep your Apple ID, email account, and Kairo account credentials protected.

9. Children’s Privacy

Kairo is not intended for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

Because Kairo relates to health and protocol tracking, it should be used only by users who are legally able to manage their own health records or with appropriate supervision and consent where required by law.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in the United States or other countries where we or our service providers operate. If information is transferred internationally, we use safeguards required by applicable law.
